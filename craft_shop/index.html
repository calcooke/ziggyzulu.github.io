Comeragh Craft

Comeragh craft is a family run business nestled at the foot of the Comeragh mountains. Founded in 1982 by Pat & Helen Cronin, they combine their love of craft with the Irish wildlife to produce high quality products. Each item is handmade and unique, as our products are made from natural materials. These are the perfect gifts to bring the character of the Irish landscape with you wherever you go.
