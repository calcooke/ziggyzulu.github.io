Suppliers

Coopershill is one of the many partnerships we have with deer farms throughout the country. Farms such as Coopershill, Kildalton and Mitchelstown ensure we have materials to work with through the lull in hunting seasons.

 
 

We have a close relationship with the IDS, providing us with a direct link to the hunters that make materials largely available. As a result of the yearly cull, a large amount of resources are made available. Through our partnership with the IDS we buy directly from trusted, licenced and professional hunters, acting within the law and humane restraints.

 
 

Advantage Engineering is a steel company based in Co. Waterford. Their high grade steel provides the quality blades used in our range of knives.
