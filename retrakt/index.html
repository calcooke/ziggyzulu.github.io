RETRAKT RECORDS



Retrakt records is an Irish music label/collective formed in 2014 by Noel Hogan, Paul McLoone and Gavin Glass

At RETRAKT we keep a diverse genre crossing roster, choosing the cream of the crop from each. Our tightly knit community keeps the gears in motion and work with our roster to push them to their potential.

RETRAKT was described by NME as "one of the best new music labels of recent years, and one to watch in 2016."
