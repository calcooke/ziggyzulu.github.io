I am POP

Cork duo "I am POP" are masters of infectious pop and RnB. Comprised of producer Cian Ryan and vocalist Senita Makupe, their 2014 debut EP "p0p k1ller$" strongly established their presence on both the Irish and Uk scene. 2015 brought the release of their first album "Metroid singsong" on Retrakt records, carrying the duo further in their adventure.

Feathers

Feathers is a one man project by vocalist & producer Owen Lewis. His soaring vocals combined with spacious glacial instrumentals are most unique, collecting him many awards in his short career. With two successful albums under his belt already on Retrakt records he's poised for a long and interesting career. His third album "Flint" launches this summer.

Bart Graft

Bart Graft is the definitive master of 80's retro synthwave. Amassing a huge internet following in his early days, Retrakt have brought him further into his career with 2 albums on our roster already. Bart Graft never fails to produce album after album of catchy hits and shows no sign of slowing down.

Saami

Saami are a most unusual artpop duo. A combination of perfectly crafted beats by producer Jari Hahto, amazingly unique vocals by Tarja Jansen, and a never ending rotation of stage dress have propelled them to the forefront of the Irish & UK scenes. Their live show is a festival favourite that simply must not be missed.

Fire Toy

Fire Toy is comprised of producer Niall Markham. His cinematic approach to music makes the perfect backdrop to almost any circumstance. With 3 albums under his belt and numerous productions for soundtracks, his diverse career is on a steady course into the future. We've welcomed him on Retrakt records for his third album - "Shiva".
